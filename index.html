About Us

Zimbabwe Mining Rights Trade Center is the nation's premier marketplace for mining claims and mineral rights. With over 15 years of industry experience, we specialize in connecting claim owners with serious buyers and investors.

Our team includes geologists, legal experts, and mining professionals who ensure every transaction is compliant with Zimbabwe's mining regulations and represents fair market value.
